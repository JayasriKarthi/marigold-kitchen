Our story

We cook the way we eat at home — just louder.

Marigold started as a Sunday lunch that ran too long and fed too many people. We kept the part that mattered: a crowded table, hands reaching across dishes, and someone asking for the recipe.

Everything is made in-house, from the masalas we roast each morning to the kulfi we churn at night. We source from growers across Tamil Nadu and pick produce for how bright it tastes, not how long it keeps.
